# Top 10 Portfolio Layout Inspirations (2024-2025)

This document compiles the most viewed and highly-rated developer portfolios from platforms like **Awwwards**, **Behance**, and curated industry lists. Use these as a reference for layout, animation, and interaction strategies during our React/Vite migration.

---

### 1. [Bruno Simon](https://brunosimon.com/)
*   **Status:** Awwwards "Site of the Year" winner.
*   **Key Highlights:** 
    *   **Gamified UX:** Interactive 3D world built with **Three.js**.
    *   **Navigation:** Driving a toy car to different "islands" representing sections (About, Projects, etc.).
    *   **Takeaway:** Great for showing off high-end WebGL skills, though high-effort.

### 2. [Dennis Snellenberg](https://dennissnellenberg.com/)
*   **Status:** Multiple Awwwards "Site of the Day" and high Behance engagement.
*   **Key Highlights:** 
    *   **Smooth Motion:** Incredible use of **GSAP** for scrolling and page transitions.
    *   **Magnetic Elements:** Buttons and cursors that "stick" to the mouse.
    *   **Takeaway:** The benchmark for "high-end freelance" aesthetic—clean typography with premium motion.

### 3. [Mees Verberne](https://meesverberne.com/)
*   **Status:** Awwwards Portfolio Honors (2024).
*   **Key Highlights:** 
    *   **Creative Front-end:** Glitch effects, banded gradients, and bold typography.
    *   **Identity:** A very strong "digital-first" personal brand.
    *   **Takeaway:** Excellent inspiration for a unique, non-standard visual identity.

### 4. [Corentin Bernadou](https://corentinbernadou.com/)
*   **Status:** Awwwards Developer Award (2025 cycle).
*   **Key Highlights:** 
    *   **Case Study Driven:** Deep dives into projects with high-end transitions.
    *   **Motion Storytelling:** Using movement to guide the user through technical achievements.
    *   **Takeaway:** Perfect for showcasing "Game Development" projects where visual impact is key.

### 5. [Adham Dannaway](https://www.adhamdannaway.com/)
*   **Status:** Classic UI/UX and Front-end benchmark.
*   **Key Highlights:** 
    *   **Dual-Identity:** The "Designer vs. Developer" toggle.
    *   **Simplicity:** Clean, high-contrast layout that focuses on the core message.
    *   **Takeaway:** Great for portfolios where you want to highlight multiple roles (e.g., Developer & Game Designer).

### 6. [Keita Yamada](https://p5aholic.me/)
*   **Status:** Highly viewed for minimalist, high-performance execution.
*   **Key Highlights:** 
    *   **Performance:** Incredibly fast loading and transitions.
    *   **Technical Focus:** Focuses on "Experiments" (P5.js, shaders) rather than just projects.
    *   **Takeaway:** Inspiration for a "clean & technical" look that prioritizes speed and raw code.

### 7. [Elliott Mangham](https://elliottmangham.com/)
*   **Status:** Awwwards Portfolio Honors (Nov 2025).
*   **Key Highlights:** 
    *   **Bento Box Layout:** Organizing skills and projects into a clean, tile-based grid.
    *   **Micro-interactions:** Subtle hover states and scroll-triggered animations.
    *   **Takeaway:** The most "modern-standard" layout for 2025. Very professional and readable.

### 8. [Artiom Yakushev](https://yakushev.design/)
*   **Status:** Awwwards Site of the Day (Dec 2025).
*   **Key Highlights:** 
    *   **Immersive WebGL:** Scroll-responsive 3D environments.
    *   **Math-heavy UI:** Demonstrates a high level of procedural animation and shader knowledge.
    *   **Takeaway:** High-end inspiration for a "Technical Artist" or "Creative Developer" portfolio.

### 9. [Sharon Yi](https://sharonyi.com/)
*   **Status:** Featured in 2025 "Top Developer" lists.
*   **Key Highlights:** 
    *   **Personality:** Playful, emoji-rich, and colorful.
    *   **Modern Accessibility:** High contrast and clear hierarchy despite the "fun" aesthetic.
    *   **Takeaway:** Inspiration for breaking the "boring resume" mold.

### 10. [Kenneth Jimmy](https://kennethjimmy.com/)
*   **Status:** Recognized for unique UX and performance.
*   **Key Highlights:** 
    *   **Boxed Layout:** Feels like navigating a physical document or a terminal.
    *   **Outside-the-box UI:** Breaks the standard full-width scroll pattern.
    *   **Takeaway:** Great for a "terminal" or "retro-modern" aesthetic.

---

## 2025 Trends to Consider
*   **Bento Grids:** Use tiles for projects and skills.
*   **GSAP / Framer Motion:** Mandatory for "premium" feel.
*   **Typography:** Bold, oversized headers (e.g., Montserrat or custom display fonts).
*   **Dark Mode:** Deep blacks (#0a0a0a) with vibrant accent colors.
